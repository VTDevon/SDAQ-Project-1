Background

EventSphere is an online event management and ticketing platform designed to serve both event organizers and attendees effectively. This platform facilitates seamless organization, ticketing, and management of events, offering a comprehensive solution tailored to these core users. Organizers can create and manage events, track ticket sales in real time, offer merchandise, and facilitate in-app food and drink sales.

For attendees, EventSphere provides an integrated experience from ticket purchase to event participation. Users can buy tickets, pre-order items, and utilize a unique QR code to check in and collect their pre-ordered merchandise or concessions. Vendors, forming a subcomponent for organizers, have easy access to attendee orders, fulfilling them through QR scans and real-time inventory updates, thus ensuring efficient service.

The platform operates on a microservices architecture to support scalability and real-time updates across essential services, including event management, ticketing, order processing, and vendor management. Core services include a ticketing service for QR code generation and attendee check-ins, an order management service for merchandise and food handling, and an analytics service that offers organizers post-event insights.

Through this design, EventSphere aims to transform event management by providing a streamlined online platform for organizers and attendees alike.
